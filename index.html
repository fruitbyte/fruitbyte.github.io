Hi, my name is Sonia Wong

a second year Integrated Engineering student at the University Of British Columbia, interested in aviation, unmanned aircraft systems, and robotics.

LinkedIn Autobud UAS

From grade three I was drawn to all sorts of mechanisms- I collected wind-up toys and dissected them, and spent my weekends in the basement building basic machines out of Lego such as a small vending machine. When I joined Air Cadets at the age of 12, my passion for aviation grew and the concept of flight became more tangible. A few short summer camps, weekly ground school and classes, two six-week credited Aviation Technology courses, and a private pilot's license later, I am now aiming to be at the forefront developing the future of aviation and aviation technology.
